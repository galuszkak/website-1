Being a great engineer takes much more than writing code. Soft Skills Engineering is a weekly podcast for software developers who want to level up their engineering skills outside the realm of writing code. Our hosts, Jamison Dance and Dave Smith, are here to make you laugh and make you wise as they answer your questions, dear listeners, on the air each week.

If you would like us to answer your question on the air, send us a private message on Twitter.
